Darasi na 8: Semantic HTML

Tsara Shafin Yanar Gizo da HTML Tags

👨‍🏫 Malami:

A yau za mu koyi Semantic HTML. Wato amfani da tags da suke bayyana ma'anar sassan website.

👨‍🎓 Ɗalibi:

Malam, menene bambancin Semantic HTML da sauran tags?

👨‍🏫 Malami:

Semantic tags suna gaya wa browser da mai karatu aikin kowane sashe. Misali: header yana nuna saman shafi, footer yana nuna ƙarshen shafi.

Muhimman Semantic Tags

Tag Aiki
<header> Sashen saman website.
<nav> Wurin links na kewayawa.
<main> Babban bayanin shafi.
<section> Raba shafi zuwa sassa.
<article> Bayanin kansa ko labari.
<aside> Ƙarin bayani a gefe.
<footer> Ƙarshen website.
